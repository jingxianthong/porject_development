English Lesson 1


Kindness, though seemingly insignificant, holds immense power. A smile exchanged with a stranger, a comforting word during tough times, or a simple act of holding the door open—all contribute to a ripple effect of positivity. These small gestures create connections, brighten days, and remind us of our shared humanity. In a world often chaotic and divided, let us embrace kindness as our superpower. It costs nothing, yet its impact is immeasurable. So, let us be kind, for in doing so, we change the world—one small act at a time.
